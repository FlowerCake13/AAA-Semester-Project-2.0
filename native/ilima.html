ʻIlima

English Name: Black Coral

Genus: Sida fallax

Indigenous vs Endemic

Information!

‘Ilima was used as a mild laxative for babies. However it is also used to make a floral lei. ‘Ilima is a shrub that can grow up to around eight feet. It also became ‘Oahu’s state flower became 1923.
